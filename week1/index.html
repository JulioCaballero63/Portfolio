  • Watched the Master Mobile UX video

    Notes:

    • Responsive retrofitting does does not equal mobile first.
    • Do mobile first! do not try to shove a marshmallow into a piggy bank.
    • Slow loading apps loose money. I should reduce image size, optimize CSS and JS files, and GZIP files to eliminate unnecessary data. This will help reduce loading time.
    • Reachability is very important, on mobile devices moving the primary controls (menu) to the bottom of the screen increases reachability.
    • Many user do not know what a hamburger menu is, the word menu make to look like a button is the best option.
    • Make the app easy to understand and use and focus on the most important things first.
    • Design intuitive and mobile friendly forms. Make labels into float labels (display inside the field) and make inputs bigger.
    • If using pagination to complete forms, make sure to include a progress indicator.
    • Masking passwords doesn't even increase security, but it does cost business due to login failures.
    • Display the correct keyboard for the type of data that the user needs to enter.
    • Do not use pop ups/pop overs, in between adds or auto play music and audio. Keep it simple and make sure adds are unobtrusive, the content first!
